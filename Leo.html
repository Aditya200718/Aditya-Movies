WELCOME TO ADITYA MOVIES

LEO

Cast - Thalapathy Vijay, Sanjay Dutt, Arjun Sarja, Trisha Krishnan, Madonna Sebastian, Gautham Vasudev Menon, Mysskin and Others

Director - Lokesh Kanagaraj

Genre - Action, Thriller

Release Date - October 19, 2023

Storyline - In the peaceful hills of Himachal Pradesh, Parthiban leads a quiet life running a café and rescuing wild animals. But his hidden past surfaces when a violent café attack forces him to kill, drawing media attention and suspicion. Soon, underworld figures Anthony and Harold Das insist Parthiban is actually Leo Das, their lost son and a deadly gangster. As the mystery deepens, Parthiban must face brutal truths and protect his family while confronting a past that refuses to stay buried. A gritty tale of identity, survival, and redemption.
